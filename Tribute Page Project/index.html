The Story of Smaug

Smaug was the last great Fire Drake of Middle Earth. Cold Drakes lack the ability of Fire Drakes as they breathe out ice.

For 170+ years, Smaug had lived under the Lonely Mountain where the vast treasure of the Durin's Folk were. Dragons are very attracted to treasures.

The settlement of the dragons (before Smaug's arrival) drove out the Dwarves, hence pushing Thorin (Dwarf Prince) to come back for revenge to become the rightful King Under The Mountain.

The Company of Thorin employed the help of Bilbo Baggins (a Hobbit) to retrieve the Arkenstone (a.k.a. "The Heart of the Mountain") because Dragons are too familiar with the scent of Dwarves but a Hobbit's scent might slip by unnoticed.

If a dragon had smelled the scent of a Dwarf, it would instantly torch the living beard out of one.

When Bilbo came face to face with Smaug, he tries to distract him with flattery and riddles (pro dragon tip: they love wasting time solving riddles), but Smaug grew tiresome and decides to burn Lake Town (the nearby village).

Bard, the bearer of The Black Arrow (the only thing that can pierce the armor of a dragon and send it to its demise) shoots Smaug down and successfully kills the dragon once and for all.
